Featured Projects



NYC School Monitor

A Directory of 400+ NYC High Schools where users can publish and share reviews. Think of it this way, there are number of sites for reviewing restaurants but there aren't any apps that lets parents and students leave reviews on our public education system. See the problem here? Now you can read all about that school down the block before sending your kids there for the next four years. (yikes)

Tech Stack:

React, Redux, Ruby on Rails API, NYC Open Data API, Google Maps API, PostgreSQL, Active Record Rack Cors, Active Model Serializer, BCrypt, JWT, Semantic UI, HTML, CSS



Netflax

Are you a movie junkie? Do you love spending free time watching the latest K-Dramas or documentaries? Netflax is a Movie/TV Show Searching App where users can browse through thounsands of shows based on genres. It uses the MovieDB api to get the latest movies and shows available for streaming. Inspired by the popular streaming service, IYKYK.

Tech Stack:

React JS, Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Rack-cors, Active Model Serializers, Rest-Client API, HTML, CSS, Active Record



Life After Covid

Single page e-commerce web app that allows users to purchase PPE products. The inspiration of this app came while brainstorming for ideas help people cope through this diffcult time during COVID. The products displayed on the app have become a neccessary part of our every day lives.

Tech Stack:

JavaScript, Rails API, PostgreSQL, Active Record, Rack Cors, Active Model Serializers, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS



Coterie

Looking for ways to stay connected with people during a pandemic? Coterie lets users host and join virtual meetings to mingle with people with shared interests. Browse through popular topics and categories and join the fun! The word Coterie means a small group of people with shared interests or tastes.

Tech Stack:

Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Active Record, BCrypt



Thats What Who Said

Are you an Office Fan? Need a motivating (or demotivating) quote to get through a long day? Try out this random quote generator that pulls actual quotes from the popular TV show, The Office. Then share the fun with your friends on Twitter! The app fetches data from theOffice API.
